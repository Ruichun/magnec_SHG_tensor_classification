SHG and LMO of 32 original and 32 gray magnetic point groups

Note:

(1) MPG: magnetic point group, LMO: linear magneto-optic effect. (2) the subscript number of SHG tensor means the independent element number. (3) there is only the even part (χeven) for gray MPGs (the second one of each row, with 1′), and the odd and even parts ((χeven) and χodd) have same symmetry in original MPGs (the first one of each row, without 1′).
